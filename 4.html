Hwasa is the member of Mamamoo. Her birthday is July 23th.

Ahn Hye-jin (born July 23, 1995),better known by her stage name Hwasa, is a South Korean singer, songwriter and rapper signed under RBW Entertainment.

She is a member of South Korean girl group Mamamoo. In February 2019, Hwasa made her debut as a solo artist with the digital single "Twit".
