Solar is the member of Mamamoo. Her birthday is February 21th.

Kim Yong-sun (born February 21, 1991),better known by the mononym Solar, is a South Korean singer, songwriter and actress.

She is known as the leader and vocalist of South Korean girl group Mamamoo.
